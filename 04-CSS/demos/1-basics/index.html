1. About Moonlight Pizza Co.

Welcome to Moonlight Pizza Co., a fictional neighborhood pizza spot created to help you learn HTML. We may not exist in real life, but your code does!

Our “story” is simple: crispy crust, fresh toppings, and a menu that’s easy to read for humans and browsers. This section shows how you can use headings, paragraphs, and semantic structure to introduce a business.
